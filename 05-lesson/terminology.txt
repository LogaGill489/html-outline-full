Some notes about page layout and where to collect images:

The page that users can see follow similar naming conventions to that of newspapers:
The "Crease" is the area at the bottom of screen.
The "Fold" is the area of screen, i.e. the part folded so that you can't see it.

Images and Loading:
The loading attribute in an image is used to help with performance,
use lazy when it is about to show an image beyond the fold.
